About This Portfolio

This portfolio showcases analytical work framed as preliminary research investigations into agricultural systems, climate risk, and production efficiency. Each project is structured around a research question, an analytical methodology, and insights that could inform deeper academic inquiry or policy evaluation. The intent is to demonstrate rigorous data interpretation, quantitative modeling, and evidence synthesis.

Rainfall Variability and Its Effect on Maize Yield in Zambia

This study evaluates how rainfall variability influences maize yield across ten provinces of Zambia. The analysis tests whether total seasonal rainfall explains yield variation, or whether provincial differences and rainfall patterns provide stronger explanatory value.
